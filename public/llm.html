Frequently Asked Questions

What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

Our standard MOQ is 500 kg to 1 ton depending on the product. For new customers, we can discuss smaller trial orders to establish the business relationship.

What are your payment terms?

We typically work with 50% deposit upon order confirmation and 50% balance before shipment. We also accept Letter of Credit (L/C) for larger orders.

How long is the lead time?

Standard lead time is 2-3 weeks from order confirmation and deposit receipt. For larger orders or custom specifications, it may take 3-4 weeks.

Do you offer organic certification?

Yes, most of our products are USDA Organic and EU Organic certified. We can provide all necessary certification documents for import purposes.

Can you do private label/OEM packaging?

Absolutely! We offer full private label and OEM services. You can provide your own packaging design or work with our team to create custom packaging.

What countries do you export to?

We export to over 18 countries including USA, Canada, Germany, Netherlands, UK, Japan, South Korea, Australia, UAE, and more.

Can I request samples before ordering?

Yes, we encourage all potential buyers to request samples. Product samples are free, but courier costs (DHL/FedEx) are borne by the buyer.

What quality documents do you provide?

We provide Certificate of Analysis (COA), phytosanitary certificates, organic certificates, bill of lading, packing list, commercial invoice, and any other documents required for import.

What is the transit time to USA/Europe?

Ocean freight: USA West Coast ~25-30 days, Europe (Rotterdam/Hamburg) ~28-35 days. Air freight is available at 3-5 days for urgent orders.

Do you offer CIF/CNF pricing?

Yes, we support multiple Incoterms including FOB, CIF, CNF, and Ex-Works. Contact us for CIF/CNF quotes to your destination port.
